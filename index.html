Key Insights

Critical findings from the Pacific connectivity data analysis

Digital Divide

French Polynesia leads with 88.5% internet usage, while other nations lag significantly behind.

Broadband Infrastructure

Most Pacific nations have fewer than 5 broadband subscriptions per 100 inhabitants.

E-Government Progress

Fiji shows the strongest e-government performance with an E-Government Index of 0.62.

Mobile Coverage

4G coverage varies dramatically, with Samoa at 99% and French Polynesia at 90%.
